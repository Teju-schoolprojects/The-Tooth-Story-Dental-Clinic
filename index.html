FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Got dental queries? We have compiled answers to common concerns regarding root canals, dental implants, and sterilization.

Is root canal treatment (RCT) painful at your clinic?

At The Tooth Story, we prioritize painless procedures. We use modern local anesthetics combined with advanced digital laser dentistry equipment. Most patients report feeling absolutely no pain during their RCT and experience immediate relief from prior tooth aches.

What is the benefit of getting dental implants?

Dental implants are permanent fixtures that function exactly like natural teeth. Unlike removable dentures, they prevent jawbone loss, do not slip or shift during speaking or eating, and last a lifetime if maintained with regular hygiene checks.

How do you ensure sterilization and patient safety?

Patient safety is our highest priority. All reusable tools undergo strict multi-stage ultrasonic cleaning and autoclave sterilization. Disposable items are used wherever possible, and our treatment rooms are sanitised after every single patient consultation.

Are your dental laboratory products reliable?

Yes. All our ceramic crowns, bridges, and implant prosthetics are manufactured by certified premium labs, primarily Dentcare Laboratories, ensuring precise fit, lifelike aesthetics, and durable quality cards.
